Description

Who:

Anyone looking to find out about community events, weather, or news. This could include locals, anyone planning their day around the weather, looking to book a vacation, or preparing for upcoming weather events.

Demographics:

This site is not restrictive to age, race ethnicity, income, gender, income, religion, marital status, education, or employment.

User Device:

This site will be responsive to various devices including: Smartphone, Tablet, and Desktop
